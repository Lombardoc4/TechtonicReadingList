Techtonic Reading List


Techtonic is a show/podcast on a NJ local radio station, WFMU, hosted by Mark Hurst. He describes his show as,
"Conversations with creators and thinkers who are charting the way forward in a tech-saturated society. Tech, community, video games, and whatever else is next."


This reading list is composed of all books discusses on the show.
